About

The Sovereign Autocratic Totalitarian Dicatorship of Lectulus & Cathedra was founded on the 29th of August, 2024. We claim sovereignty over the small, debated three-seater couch west of Mrs Templeton's office and left of the student printers in the St Laurence's Library. We are a Socialist Democratic Constitutional Monarchy. Since most countries with 'Republic' or 'Democratic' in their name are not, we decided in order to prevent confusing anyone our name should include the opposites of those words. Our current monarch is Her Gracious Majesty, Empress Mrs Templeton. The government is influenced by the Roman Republic and has two elected officials with equal power known as The President and The Chancellor.
